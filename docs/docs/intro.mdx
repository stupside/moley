Share a local app on your own domain in one command. Moley unlocks the good parts of Cloudflare — [Tunnels](https://developers.cloudflare.com/cloudflare-one/connections/connect-networks/), DNS, [Access](https://developers.cloudflare.com/cloudflare-one/policies/access/) — from a single YAML file. Point it at `localhost:3000`, declare who can see it, get a live URL.
